Adopt-a-Buttler

Good day! May I clean up your city's butts?

Adopt-a-buttler is a solution to cigarette litter. These friendly, stylish Buttlers want nothing more than your butts in their faces. By sponsoring a Buttler in your neighborhood you can support cleaner streets and help to turn peoples' bad habits into useful products.

To adopt your very own Buttler, please email info@adoptabuttler.com.
